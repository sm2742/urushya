# Urushya

### Change theme
- Click the **Logo**

### Make a call
- Enter a **Peer ID** to connect to the service
- Enter your friend's *Peer ID* as **Remote ID**
- Click on **Connect** to chat with them
- Click on **Call** to start a call

### Record video
- *Click* on the video to take a **Snapshot**
- *Right click* on the video to start/stop **recording**

### Bot actions keywords
- **Encrypt** or **Decrypt** with a *file*
- **Upload** with a *file* (if logged in)
- **My IP** to get your IP info
- **Check IP** followed by an *IP address* to get its info

> Screen recording may not work on mobile devices

> Face cam button may not work on desktop

> All file transfers are encrypted by your **Encryption Key**

> Encrypted files are larger in size

> Connection only works when both peers are online

> If a media stream is on when connecting on a call, the running stream will be shared on call
